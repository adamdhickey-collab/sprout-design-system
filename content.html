Frequently Asked Questions

When is the next billing period?expand_more

Your next billing period begins on the first day of the month following your activation date. Invoices are issued on the same date and reflect any usage from the prior cycle.

How can I set up auto pay?expand_more

Go into your Profile and under Payment Settings select the Set up auto pay button to enroll in automatic payments on your bills.

Where can I download my statements?expand_more

Statements for the last 24 months live under Account · Billing · Statements. Each statement is available as PDF or CSV.
